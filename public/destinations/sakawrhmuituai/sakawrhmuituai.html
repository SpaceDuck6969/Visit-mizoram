Sakawrhmuituai Sunset - A New Year Experience in Mizoram

For the first time, the Tourism Department of Mizoram organised a special year-end celebration called “Sakawrhmuituai Sunset” at the scenic Sakawrhmuituai Hill near Sihphir village, close to Aizawl. Held on the final evening of the year, the event attracted a large gathering of local residents and visitors from outside the state, all eager to witness one of Mizoram’s most stunning sunset views. The programme highlighted the importance of community based tourism, with strong participation and support from the local community. The success of the event demonstrated how collaboration between villagers and tourism authorities can transform a natural location into a vibrant travel experience.

A Scenic Celebration

Sakawrhmuituai Hill is known for its:

  • Comfortable cottages
  • Picnic spots
  • Camping Areas
  • Open grasslands for relaxation

It serves as the perfect base for exploring nearby villages and enjoying authentic rural Mizo life.

Why Visit Sakawrhmuituai Hill?

  • Located around 20-25 km from Aizawl
  • Easily accessible by road
  • Perfect for sunset lovers and nature enthusiasts

Why Visit Reiek

Reiek is peaceful, scenic, and culturally rich. Whether you are looking for adventure, culture, relaxation, or eco-tourism, this village offers an experience that feels both refreshing and meaningful.

If you are searching for an offbeat destination in Northeast India, Reiek Village deserves a spot on your travel list.
